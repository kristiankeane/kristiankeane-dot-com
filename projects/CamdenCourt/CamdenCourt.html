The Camden Court iOS infomational app was coded from the ground up to be used by their sales force to book conference rooms, educate prospective guests and showcase hotel amenities. The user can learn about the hotel though an interactive 3d room browser, email the hotel with questions, book a room or explore the area via maps. I coded the app according to their design, functionality and UI requirements. Link to App Home This is an enterprise app and is not available from the App Store
