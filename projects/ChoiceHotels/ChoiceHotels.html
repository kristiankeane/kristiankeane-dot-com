This portfolio app for Choice Hotels was created for their salespeople to take to their various clients to show off the different hotels and amenities related to each. The user can browse image galleries, 3-D conference/function room renderings and learn more about each hotel in the Choice Hotel Family.Choice Hotel WebsiteThis is an enterprise app and is not available from the App Store
